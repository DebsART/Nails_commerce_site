ABOUT US

Nail Vogue was founded by Augustus Romano in 2020 during the world struck pandemic. We were founded on the idea of beauty and aesthetics made easy in a time of social reclusivity. A time where women could not enjoy, thrive or express their femininity through their favorite recreational and leisure activities with other women, such as salon visits and more. We wanted to empower women even with the tumultous novelty of the times, while encouraging safe distancing. Now Nail Vogue has grown into an expressive form of selfcare, empowering women on the go! This as well as those who wouldn't normally be able to afford extensive feminine care and leisure. We offer safer and easier alternatives to nurture your nail health while still rocking the most stylist designs. Try our easy readymade nail art today!
